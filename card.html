Jellyfish

They have limited control over movement, but can use their hydrostatic skeleton to navigate through contraction-pulsations of the bell-like body.

  • Scientific Name: Medusozoa
  • Diameter: 9.8 to 20 inches
  • Average Lifespan: 12 months
  • Rank: Subphylum

Jellyfish or jellies are the major non-polyp form of individuals of the phylum Cnidaria. They are typified as free-swimming marine animals consisting of a gelatinous umbrella-shaped bell and trailing tentacles.
